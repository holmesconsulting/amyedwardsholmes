About Amy

She previously served as the Executive Director of the Bloomberg Center for Government Excellence at Johns Hopkins University, where she led efforts to empower public sector organizations across 37 countries to enhance data capacity, leverage AI, and improve public services. Her leadership has driven the development of innovative platforms like City AI Connect, which was recognized by Fast Company in 2024 for fostering responsible AI use in government.

>Prior to joining Johns Hopkins, Amy held key leadership positions at the U.S. Department of the Treasury, where she oversaw the financial operations for the entire federal government and led initiatives to enhance data transparency, including the implementation of the Digital Accountability and Transparency Act (DATA Act). Her work established USAspending.gov, the first government-wide digital product based on agile development principles.
